# TMS — Treasury Management System

Software corporate treasury runs on top of bank rails. Multi-bank aggregation.

## Vendors

- **Kyriba** — cloud leader, SaaS
- **GTreasury**
- **FIS Quantum** — enterprise
- **ION Treasury** (Wallstreet Suite, Reval, IT2)
- **SAP Treasury / S/4 HANA Cash Mgmt** — for SAP shops

## Functions

- Bank connectivity (SWIFT, [[api]], H2H)
- Cash positioning and forecasting
- Payment factory + workflow / approvals
- FX trading, hedging exposure mgmt
- Investment / debt mgmt
- Bank fee analysis ([[account-analysis]])

## Why corps use TMS not just bank portals

- Multi-bank — single pane of glass
- Workflow / approvals across bank silos
- Audit trail, SOX compliance
- Replace spreadsheets
