# TWINT

Swiss mobile P2P / P2M payments. Bank consortium product.

## Owners

- Joint venture: UBS, [[postfinance]], Raiffeisen, ZKB, cantonal banks
- Universal coverage via owner banks

## Use cases

- P2P transfers
- Merchant payments (QR at till, in-app, e-commerce)
- Vending, parking, charity collection
- Instant settlement to bank account

## Tech

- App on iOS/Android, linked to user's bank account
- QR-based POS, NFC roadmap
- Backend settles via SIC

## Why it matters for cash mgmt

- Corporate merchants accept TWINT, reconcile via bank
- Reporting feeds into [[03-tech-integration]] camt files
- TWINT-only data fields in Swiss bank statements
