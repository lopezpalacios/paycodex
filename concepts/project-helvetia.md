# Project Helvetia (Swiss wholesale CBDC)

SNB + BIS Innovation Hub + SIX. Wholesale CHF CBDC pilot on SIX Digital Exchange (SDX).

## Phases

- **Phase I (2020)** — proof of concept: integrate wholesale CBDC into SIX SDX
- **Phase II (2022)** — settle real DLT-based securities with wholesale CBDC; integrate with [[../operators/snb]] T-account and SIC
- **Phase III (2023)** — live pilot: real wholesale CBDC issued by SNB to commercial banks for DLT securities settlement on SDX
- **Phase IV (extended into 2027)** — continued live operation, expanded use cases

## Why it matters

- First live wholesale CBDC on production systems by major central bank
- Settlement of digital securities atomic in CB money
- CH sets pace globally

## Mechanics

- Wholesale CBDC tokens on SDX DLT
- Backed 1:1 by SIC reserves at SNB
- Banks subscribe to tokens via SDX
- Used for DvP on tokenized bonds, equities
