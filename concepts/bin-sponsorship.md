# BIN sponsorship

Bank lends BIN (Bank Identification Number) to non-bank issuer for card programs. Foundation of card-based BaaS.

## Mechanics

- Card scheme ([[card-schemes]]) issues BIN to licensed bank
- Bank "sponsors" non-bank program manager (fintech)
- Fintech brand front, bank legal issuer
- Bank holds funds, takes regulatory responsibility
- Fintech does customer experience, marketing, app

## Examples

- Sutton Bank → Cash App debit
- Stride Bank → Stripe Issuing US
- Pathward → Affirm / others
- Solaris BIN → many EU fintechs

## Bank's role

- KYC / AML oversight on end customers
- Sanctions screening
- Settlement with scheme
- Regulatory reporting
- Fraud + dispute backstop

## Recent stress

- 2024 Synapse collapse stranded customer funds (multi-day ops failure)
- OCC enforcement on BaaS banks for inadequate controls
- Regulators tightening sponsor oversight expectations
