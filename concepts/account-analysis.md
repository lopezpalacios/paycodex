# Account Analysis Statement

Monthly billing artifact bank sends corporate. Standard format using AFP service codes.

## Anatomy

- Per-account balance summary (average ledger, average collected, float)
- Service charges section — every transaction type, volume × unit price
- Earnings allowance section — balance × [[ecr]] × factor
- Net position — fees billed if gross > earnings credit

## AFP codes

Industry-standard (Association for Financial Professionals) catalog. Examples:

- 01 0150 — account maintenance
- 25 0100 — ACH credit originated
- 15 0100 — wire transfer outgoing domestic

Standardization lets corps benchmark across banks.

## Used for

- Bank fee analysis tools (TMS modules, Redbridge, BankNova)
- RFP benchmarking ([[01-onboarding]])
- Volume discount negotiation
