# Digital Euro

ECB's retail CBDC project. In **preparation phase** (since Nov 2023).

## Phases

- **Investigation phase**: 2021-2023
- **Preparation phase**: 2023 onwards
- **Decision to issue**: pending Eurosystem governing council, expected 2026-2027
- **Issuance**: earliest 2027-2028 if approved

## Key design points

- Two-tier: ECB issues, banks/PSPs distribute
- Holding limit (likely €3,000 per person) to prevent bank disintermediation
- Privacy: low-value offline anonymous; online standard tx records
- Free at point of use for citizens
- Pan-eurozone reach mandatory

## Bank impact

- Mandatory distribution role for credit institutions in eurozone
- New wallet product, KYC integration
- Funding shift: deposits → CBDC up to holding limit
- Compensation model debated (PSPs to be compensated for costs?)

## Legal framework

- Single-currency package proposed by Commission June 2023
- Co-legislators (EU Council + Parliament) negotiating
- Cash legal tender + digital euro legal tender
