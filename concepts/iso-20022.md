# ISO 20022

XML-based financial messaging standard. Replacing legacy SWIFT MT and many domestic formats.

## Key message types

- **pain.001** — customer credit transfer initiation (replaces MT101)
- **pain.002** — payment status report
- **pacs.008** — FI-to-FI customer credit transfer (replaces MT103)
- **camt.052** — intraday statement (replaces MT942)
- **camt.053** — end-of-day statement (replaces MT940)
- **camt.054** — debit/credit notification

## Why migrate

- Richer structured data (LEI, structured remittance, purpose codes)
- Native to [[rtp-fednow]], TARGET2, CHAPS, Fedwire (Mar 2025), CHIPS
- Enables compliance automation and reconciliation

## Key dates

- Fedwire MX live: March 2025
- SWIFT cross-border MT retirement: November 2025
- Many domestic rails already migrated
