# Green deposit

Corporate deposit at bank where bank commits to using funds for green / sustainable lending or investing.

## Mechanics

- Bank labels deposit "green"
- Bank tracks corresponding green asset book
- Customer earns standard or slight premium rate (or accepts slight discount for green badge)
- Reporting: bank publishes use-of-funds annually

## Use case

- Corporate ESG report can claim portion of cash held in green deposits
- Treasury policy may mandate X% of cash in green deposits

## Standards

- No single global standard
- Internal bank methodology + audit
- Some banks align to EU Taxonomy ([[../regulations/eu-taxonomy]])
