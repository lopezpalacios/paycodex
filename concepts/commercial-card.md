# Commercial card

Card products for corporate use. Subset of [[card-schemes]].

## Types

- **P-card (purchasing card)** — for low-value supplier purchases, replaces invoice + PO flow
- **T&E card** — travel + entertainment, employee-issued
- **Fleet card** — fuel + vehicle expenses
- **Virtual card** — see [[virtual-card]] — single-use or controlled-use number for AP

## Benefits to corp

- Extended DPO (Days Payable Outstanding) — interest-free float
- Rebate income (~100-200 bps back from issuer)
- Spend visibility + controls
- Replaces small-ticket AP processing cost

## Benefits to bank (issuer)

- Interchange revenue
- Annual fees
- Float on settlement
- Cross-sell to lending + cash mgmt

## Integration

- ERP integration via daily transaction file
- Data: amount, merchant, MCC code, optional Level III (line-item detail)
- Reconciliation against expense system or AP
