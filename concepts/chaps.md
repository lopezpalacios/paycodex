# CHAPS

UK GBP RTGS. Operated by Bank of England.

## Properties

- Same-day GBP wholesale settlement
- High-value, time-critical
- Cutoff 18:00 GMT (extensions possible)
- ISO 20022 native (post-2023 migration)

## Use cases

- Property purchases (deposit + completion)
- Big B2B invoice settlement
- FX settlement leg
- Treasury wholesale

## Membership

- ~30 direct participants
- Indirect access via correspondent
