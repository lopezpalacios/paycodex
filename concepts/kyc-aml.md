# KYC / AML

Know Your Customer / Anti-Money Laundering. Foundation of every banking relationship.

## KYC components

- Identify the entity — articles, EIN, registration
- Identify beneficial owners (25%+ per FinCEN CDD rule)
- Identify control persons (senior officer)
- Verify identities — documents + screening
- Risk rate — industry, geography, products, ownership
- Periodic refresh (1-3 years by risk tier)

## AML program

- BSA / FinCEN compliance (US), AMLD (EU), FATF globally
- Transaction monitoring — rules + ML detection
- SAR (Suspicious Activity Report) filing
- CTR (Currency Transaction Report) for cash >\$10K
- Sanctions integration — see [[ofac]]

## High-risk customer types

- MSB (money service businesses)
- Cannabis, crypto exchanges
- Foreign correspondent banks
- PEPs (politically exposed persons)
- Shell company structures

## Cost

Big bank AML programs: 1000s of staff, \$100M+ annual. Major fine driver across industry.
