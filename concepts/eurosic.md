# euroSIC

EUR clearing for Swiss banks. Run by SIX, settled in EUR via correspondent at Commerzbank Frankfurt.

## Why it exists

- CH not in eurozone — no direct T2 access for CH banks (mostly)
- Swiss banks need EUR clearing for cross-border CH↔EU flows
- euroSIC = workaround infra giving SEPA-like reach

## Reach

- SCT, SCT Inst, SDD via euroSIC's bridge to SEPA
- Some Swiss banks are direct SEPA participants — euroSIC for the rest
