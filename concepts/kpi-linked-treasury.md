# KPI-linked treasury products (umbrella)

Range of products linked to ESG KPIs, beyond loans + bonds.

## Examples

- **ESG-linked SCF** (supply chain finance) — supplier discount rate tied to supplier's ESG score
- **ESG-linked credit lines / RCF**
- **ESG-linked deposits**
- **ESG-linked FX derivatives** — pricing tied to corporate ESG (rare)

## Treasury implications

- KPI tracking infra needed
- Annual / quarterly measurement
- Auditor verification
- Greenwashing risk if KPIs not stretching

## Vendor / data

- ESG data: MSCI, Sustainalytics, ISS, Refinitiv
- Internal sustainability data (emissions, social metrics)
