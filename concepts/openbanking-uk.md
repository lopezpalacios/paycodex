# Open Banking UK

UK regime, regulator-mandated since 2018. Initially CMA9 banks; expanded.

## Authority

- Open Banking Implementation Entity (OBIE) — replaced 2024 by JROC (Joint Regulatory Oversight Committee)
- FCA + PSR (Payment Systems Regulator) regulate
- Standards published by OBIE (now successor body Open Banking Limited / future entity)

## Specifications

- v3.x current
- AIS, PIS, CBPII (rare in UK)
- Variable Recurring Payments (VRP) — UK-specific, mandate-based recurring PIS

## Adoption

- ~10M users by 2024
- Strong PIS adoption in checkout (Klarna, Volt, Trustly)
- VRP rollout for sweeping use cases

## Status post-Brexit

- UK diverging from EU PSD2 over time
- VRP mandatory for sweeping (move funds between own accounts) since 2022
- Commercial VRP (broader use cases) emerging
