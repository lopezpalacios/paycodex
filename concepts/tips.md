# TIPS — TARGET Instant Payment Settlement

ECB-operated 24/7 instant settlement service for EUR. Backbone for [[sct-inst]] reachability.

## Properties

- 24/7/365, ≤10s settlement
- Settles in central bank money
- Operates on T2 platform post-consolidation
- Multi-currency capable (Sweden joined for SEK 2022, Denmark for DKK 2025)

## Role under IPR

- Pan-EU reachability layer
- Banks reach all eurozone counterparties via TIPS even if not on same private CSM
- Pricing capped — €0.002 per transaction (post-IPR cap)

## Vs [[eba-rt1]]

- TIPS = central bank operated, broad reach
- RT1 = private (EBA Clearing), older, big-bank focused
- Interoperable via TIPS bridge
