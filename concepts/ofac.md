# OFAC Screening

Office of Foreign Assets Control. US Treasury sanctions enforcement.

## Lists

- SDN (Specially Designated Nationals)
- Sectoral Sanctions Identifications (SSI)
- Foreign Sanctions Evaders
- Plus EU, UN, UK HMT, regional lists

## Screening

- Every payment, every party (originator, beneficiary, intermediaries)
- Name + DOB + address fuzzy matching
- 50% rule — entity 50%+ owned by SDN also blocked
- Hit handling: block (hold funds, report to OFAC) vs reject (return)

## Tools

- LexisNexis Bridger, FircoSoft, Accuity, Refinitiv WorldCheck
- Typically embedded in bank payment hub before release

## Penalties

Strict liability — bank fined regardless of intent. Cases routinely \$100M+ (BNP Paribas \$8.9B in 2014).
