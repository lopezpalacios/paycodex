# Bacs

UK batch direct credit + direct debit. Run by Pay.UK (operations: Vocalink/Mastercard).

## Products

- **Bacs Direct Credit** — bulk credit (payroll, supplier payments)
- **Bacs Direct Debit** — pull payments (utilities, subscriptions)

## Cycle

- Three-day cycle: submission → processing → settlement
- "Bacs day" for industry timing

## Direct Debit Guarantee

- UK consumer protection scheme
- Indemnity refund without question for unauthorized
- Service Users (creditors) carry liability
- Established 1980s, gold standard for trust
