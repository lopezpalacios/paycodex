# BPO — Bank Payment Obligation

Bank-bank irrevocable payment commitment triggered by data match in TSU (Trade Services Utility, SWIFT).

## Concept

- Buyer's bank commits to pay seller's bank on data match (not document match)
- Replaces document-driven LC with data-driven flow
- Faster + cheaper than LC

## Status

- Launched 2013 by SWIFT
- Limited adoption
- ICC URBPO 750 governing rules
- TSU service decommissioned by SWIFT 2024 — BPO largely defunct

## Why it failed

- Buyer-seller alignment hard (both banks must adopt)
- Bilateral data matching less trusted than documents in dispute
- Existing LC infra mature, switching cost high
- Tokenization + DLT now seen as more promising path
