# FPS — Faster Payments Service

UK instant credit transfer. Live since 2008 — pioneer of modern instant payments.

## Properties

- ≤2 hours guaranteed (typically <seconds)
- 24/7/365
- ≤£1M limit (raised over years)
- GBP only

## Operator

Pay.UK (scheme), Vocalink/Mastercard (infrastructure). Vocalink also owns Bacs ops.

## New Payments Architecture (NPA)

- Scheduled FPS replacement, ISO 20022 native
- Long-running programme, repeatedly delayed
- Will consolidate FPS + Bacs onto shared rail

## Properties

- Credit push, irrevocable
- ISO 20022 (NPA target), legacy proprietary FPS messages currently
- [[cop]] applies to FPS payments
