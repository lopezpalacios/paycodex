# PostFinance

Swiss Post bank. Largest Swiss retail/SME bank by account count.

## Status

- Owned by Swiss Post (federal entity)
- Regulated by FINMA
- Cannot lend directly (statutory restriction) — partners with banks for credit

## Why it matters

- Massive base of consumer + SME accounts
- Heavy QR-bill volumes — historic ISR home
- PostFinance-only scheme (legacy) faded into national [[qr-bill]] standard
- Cash mgmt offering for SMEs differs from universal banks

## Quirks

- Strong digital channel (early to APIs)
- Big consumer collections rail — utilities, insurance, gov
- Limited corporate FX vs UBS / private banks
