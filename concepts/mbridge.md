# Project mBridge

Multi-CBDC platform for cross-border wholesale payments. BIS Innovation Hub + central banks of HK, Thailand, UAE, China, Saudi Arabia (joined 2024).

## Concept

- Single shared DLT platform for multi-currency wholesale CBDC
- Direct atomic settlement between participating jurisdictions
- Bypasses correspondent chain entirely
- 2024: expanded membership, "minimum viable product" status

## Why disruptive

- Cuts cross-border payments from days to seconds
- Removes correspondent fees + FX spreads
- Geopolitically loaded — alternative to USD-correspondent + SWIFT

## CH/EU/UK relevance

- Not direct participants
- Watch space — pressure on traditional cross-border + SWIFT
- Possible response: SWIFT's own tokenization initiatives, ECB Project Agorá
