# Virtual Accounts

One real DDA, thousands of virtual account numbers underneath.

## Use case

- Each customer or invoice gets unique virtual #
- Payment to virtual #123 = invoice #123 paid
- Auto-reconciliation kills manual cash application

## Replaces

- [[lockbox]] + remittance matching
- Per-entity DDA proliferation (use one real, slice virtually)

## Where used

- AR collections (B2B and consumer)
- In-house bank for intercompany
- Fintech / marketplace platforms (segregated client funds)
