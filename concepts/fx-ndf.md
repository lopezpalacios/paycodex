# NDF — Non-Deliverable Forward

Cash-settled FX forward in non-convertible currencies. Settlement in convertible currency (USD typically).

## Why

- Currencies with capital controls or restricted convertibility
- Examples: BRL, INR, KRW, IDR, PHP, RUB (post-2022), TWD
- Hedging exposure without onshore presence

## Mechanics

- Trade reference rate published by central bank or fixing source
- At settlement: difference between agreed rate + reference rate × notional, paid in USD
- No underlying currency physically exchanged

## Risks

- Reference rate manipulation (historic ENRBL fixing scandal)
- Reg changes to fixing methodology
- Limited to traded liquidity windows
