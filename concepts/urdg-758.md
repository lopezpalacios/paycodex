# URDG 758

Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees. ICC publication. In force since 2010.

## Scope

- Governs demand BGs (international)
- 35 articles
- Counter-guarantees + counter-counter-guarantees included

## Key principles

- **Demand-based** — beneficiary's complying demand triggers payment
- **Independence** — guarantee independent of underlying contract
- **Documentary** — payment on documents matching guarantee terms

## Demand requirements

- Written demand
- Statement of breach (per guarantee)
- Compliance with guarantee terms

## Cash mgmt link

- Bank's contingent liability
- Reserve / capital allocation per guarantee
- Demand-event drives cash payout
