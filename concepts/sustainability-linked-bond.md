# Sustainability-linked bond (SLB)

Bond with coupon step-up if issuer fails to meet ESG KPIs by target date.

## Mechanics

- KPI + Sustainability Performance Target (SPT) at issuance
- Step-up: typically +25 to +50 bps coupon if SPT missed at observation date
- One-way (penalty for miss; no reward for over-achievement) — though structures vary

## Governing principles

- ICMA Sustainability-Linked Bond Principles (SLBP)
- Distinct from Green / Social / Sustainability bonds (use-of-proceeds restricted)

## Examples

- Enel 2019 — pioneering SLB
- Many EU corporates 2020-23
- Slowdown 2024 amid greenwashing concerns
