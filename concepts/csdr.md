# CSDR — Central Securities Depositories Regulation

EU Regulation 909/2014. Governs CSDs + settlement discipline. SDR = Settlement Discipline Regime within CSDR.

## Settlement Discipline Regime (SDR)

In force Feb 2022:

- **Cash penalties** for late settlement
- **Mandatory buy-in** suspended (was scheduled, deferred indefinitely)

## Cash penalty regime

- Daily penalty on failing party
- Calculated per ISIN per day
- Penalty rate by asset class (e.g., liquid equities 1bp/day, illiquid bonds 0.5bp/day)
- Settled via CSD penalty mechanism
- Penalty income flows back to non-failing counterparty

## Cash mgmt impact

- Failed settlements = cash flow disruption (no incoming, no outgoing-finalization)
- Penalties = direct P&L hit
- Drives investment in fail prediction + pre-matching
- T+1 reduction adds pressure (US T+1 May 2024; EU T+1 target 11 Oct 2027)

## Buy-in (suspended)

- Originally planned: failing seller force-bought-in by counterparty
- Industry pushed back hard
- Currently parked indefinitely
