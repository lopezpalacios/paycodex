# DLT settlement

Distributed ledger / blockchain-based settlement infrastructure for financial assets.

## Use cases

- Tokenized securities (digital bonds, fund units)
- Cross-border payments (multi-CBDC platforms)
- Tokenized deposits (commercial bank money on DLT)
- Trade finance + supply chain (asset provenance)

## Key live / regulated venues

- **SDX** — SIX Digital Exchange (CH, FINMA-authorized DLT trading + CSD)
- **D7** — Deutsche Börse digital exchange
- **SDX wholesale CBDC** — see [[project-helvetia]]
- **HSBC Orion** — DLT-based bond issuance platform
- **JPM Onyx** / **Project Guardian** — tokenized deposit / fund pilots

## Cash leg options

- **Wholesale CBDC** ([[project-helvetia]] live) — direct CB money on DLT
- **Tokenized deposits** — commercial bank money tokenized
- **Off-chain cash leg** — bridge to RTGS for settlement
- **Stablecoins** ([[../regulations/mica]]) — limited regulated use cases

## Reg framework

- CH: DLT Act (in force 2021) — DLT trading systems, ledger-based securities
- EU: DLT Pilot Regime (2023) — sandbox for DLT-based MTF / SS facility
- UK: Digital Securities Sandbox (DSS, 2024)

## Cash mgmt impact

- Programmable cash (smart contracts triggering payments)
- Atomic settlement (no settlement risk)
- 24/7 settlement potential
- Multi-currency multi-asset interoperability (if standards align)
