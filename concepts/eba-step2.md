# EBA STEP2

EBA Clearing's batch SEPA processor. Pan-EU retail clearing for [[sepa-sct]] and [[sepa-sdd]].

## Role

- Largest pan-EU retail CSM
- Handles bulk of cross-border SCT/SDD volumes
- Multi-cycle daily settlement

## Operator

EBA Clearing — bank-owned, Paris-headquartered, operates STEP2 + RT1.

## Vs domestic CSMs

- STEP2 = pan-EU
- Domestic: equensWorldline (DE/NL), STET (FR), Iberpay (ES), CSM-domestic Italy/Austria
- Banks often use domestic for in-country, STEP2 for cross-border
