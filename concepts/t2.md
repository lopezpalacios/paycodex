# T2 — Eurosystem RTGS

Replaced TARGET2 in March 2023 consolidation with T2S. Operated by ECB / Eurosystem central banks.

## Properties

- EUR RTGS, central bank money
- Wholesale + interbank settlement
- ISO 20022 native (post-March 2023 migration)
- Cutoff 18:00 CET (with extensions for specific flows)

## Components after consolidation

- **T2** — payments RTGS
- **T2S** — securities settlement (DvP)
- **TIPS** — instant payments overlay ([[tips]])
- **ECMS** — Eurosystem Collateral Management System (live 2024)
- All on shared technical platform

## Participants

- Eurozone NCBs, eurozone banks
- Some non-EZ banks via correspondent or direct

## CH context

Most Swiss banks not direct T2 participants — reach EUR via [[eurosic]] or correspondent. Some big Swiss banks have direct T2 access via subsidiaries.
