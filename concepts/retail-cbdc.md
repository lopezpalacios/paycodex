# Retail CBDC

Central bank digital currency for general public. Public-facing equivalent of physical cash.

## Properties

- Liability of central bank (same as banknotes)
- Issued + redeemed via intermediaries (banks, EMIs typically)
- Two-tier model standard: CB issues, intermediaries distribute
- Privacy + offline functionality often debated design points

## Status (as of 2026)

- **Eurozone**: [[digital-euro]] in preparation phase (decision pending 2026-2027)
- **CH**: SNB explicitly cautious — focuses on wholesale, no plans for retail
- **UK**: BoE consulting on "digital pound" — not yet decided
- **Live**: Bahamas (Sand Dollar), Jamaica (JAM-DEX), Nigeria (eNaira), China (e-CNY pilot at scale)

## Concerns

- Disintermediation of banks (deposits flow to CBDC)
- Privacy / surveillance debate
- Cross-border / capital control implications
- Cybersecurity / systemic
- Operational complexity for banks (custody + exchange)

## Bank impact

- Distribution role (wallet provision, KYC, tokenization)
- Deposit cap concerns (regulators considering holding limits)
- New wallet + account product offering
- FX / cross-currency tokenized payments
