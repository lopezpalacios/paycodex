# Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)

LC functioning as guarantee. Primarily US instrument; used globally as alternative to BG.

## Use cases

- Performance guarantee
- Financial guarantee (loan support)
- Counter-guarantee (back-to-back arrangement)

## Vs BG

- SBLC = LC under [[ucp-600]] or ISP98 (International Standby Practices)
- BG = under [[urdg-758]]
- Functionally similar; legal framework differs
- US prefers SBLC (banks limited from issuing BGs historically)
- Europe + Asia use BG more

## Properties

- Issued same as documentary LC (MT700 series)
- Drawn only on default of underlying obligation
- "Last resort" payment mechanism
