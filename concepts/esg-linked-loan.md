# ESG-linked loan / Sustainability-linked loan (SLL)

Loan whose pricing is tied to borrower's ESG performance against agreed KPIs.

## Mechanics

- KPIs agreed at signing (e.g., GHG emission reduction, gender pay parity, supply chain sustainability)
- Annual measurement, audited
- Margin step: meet KPI = -5 to -10 bps; miss = +5 to +10 bps
- Symmetric incentive structure (both up + down)

## Differs from green loan

- **Green loan** — proceeds restricted to green projects (use-of-proceeds)
- **SLL** — general purpose, pricing tied to KPIs (no use-of-proceeds restriction)
- Sustainability-linked is more flexible, broader corporate adoption

## Governing principles

- ICMA / LSTA / APLMA Sustainability-Linked Loan Principles (SLLP)
- KPIs must be: material, measurable, time-bound, ambitious vs business-as-usual

## Cash mgmt link

- Treasury monitors KPI performance + impact on margin
- Reporting cycle aligned with KPI measurement
- May require sustainability data feed integration
- Documentation effort + audit cost
