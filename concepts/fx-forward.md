# FX Forward

Agreement to exchange currencies at agreed rate on future date (>T+2).

## Use

- Hedge known future cash flows
- Lock in rate for budgeting
- Cover invoiced receivables

## Pricing

- Forward rate = spot × (1 + interest rate differential × tenor)
- Interest rate parity: arbitrage-free pricing
- Forward points = forward - spot

## Settlement

- Either physical delivery or cash settlement (NDF — see [[fx-ndf]])
- Standard tenors: 1M, 3M, 6M, 12M, 2Y
- Customizable broken dates

## Hedge accounting (IFRS 9)

- Designated as cash flow hedge or fair value hedge
- Effectiveness testing required
- See [[hedge-accounting]]
