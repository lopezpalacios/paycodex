# Wholesale CBDC

Central bank digital currency restricted to banks + financial institutions for interbank settlement. Distinct from retail CBDC (consumer-facing).

## Properties

- Tokenized form of central bank reserves
- Settlement in central bank money (same legal status as RTGS reserve balances)
- DLT-based or centrally ledgered
- Programmable (smart contracts) — key differentiator from RTGS
- Used for: securities settlement (DvP on-chain), FX (PvP atomic), tokenized asset settlement

## Why wholesale before retail

- Smaller ecosystem (banks only)
- Existing trust + reg framework
- Settlement-risk reduction visible immediately
- Less politically charged than retail

## Key live / pilot programmes

- **[[project-helvetia]]** — SNB + BIS Innovation Hub + SIX, CHF wholesale CBDC for DLT securities (live since 2023, extended into 2027)
- **[[mbridge]]** — multi-CBDC platform (HK, Thailand, UAE, China, Saudi Arabia)
- **Project Agorá** — BIS + 7 central banks (incl ECB, BoE, BoJ, NY Fed) — tokenized cross-border + correspondent
- **Project Mariana** — SNB + Banque de France + MAS — multi-CBDC FX
- **Project Cedar** (NY Fed) — wholesale tokenized FX
- **Project Jura** — SNB + Banque de France — cross-border CHF/EUR wholesale CBDC

## Cash mgmt implications

- Wholesale leg of treasury operations may shift to CBDC rails
- Atomic settlement reduces principal risk in FX + securities
- Programmable money enables conditional payments (escrow, milestone)
- Coexistence with traditional rails for foreseeable future
