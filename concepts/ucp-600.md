# UCP 600

Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits. ICC publication. In force since 2007 (replaced UCP 500).

## Scope

- Governs LCs issued worldwide (banks adopt by reference in LC text)
- 39 articles covering issuance, amendment, presentation, examination, payment

## Key principles

- **Autonomy** — LC is independent of underlying contract
- **Strict compliance** — banks deal in documents, not goods
- **Fraud exception** — courts only intervene in clear fraud cases

## Document examination

- ISBP 745 (International Standard Banking Practice) is companion guide
- Banks have 5 banking days to examine
- Discrepancies: refuse + notify, hold docs at applicant's disposal
- Common discrepancies: late shipment, late presentation, inconsistent data, missing endorsements

## eUCP

- Companion for electronic presentation
- Limited adoption — paper still dominates

## Cash mgmt link

- Drives LC operations workflow + SLAs
- Discrepancy rate = key ops metric
- Drives bank fee economics
