# Fedwire & CHIPS — US wire rails (legacy reference)

US-only context — kept for cross-border US correspondent banking touchpoints.

## Fedwire Funds

- Fed-operated RTGS, all US banks
- Cutoff 18:00 ET (extensions possible)
- ISO 20022 migration completed March 2025

## CHIPS

- Clearing House Interbank Payments System
- Privately operated (TCH), netted settlement
- Big-bank dominated (~45 participants)
- Cutoff 17:00 ET

## When CH/EU bank touches it

- USD correspondent leg of cross-border wires
- Treasury USD nostro accounts at US banks
- Subject to OFAC even when bank is non-US
