# RTP & FedNow

US instant payment rails. 24/7/365, irrevocable, credit-push only.

## RTP

- Operator: The Clearing House (TCH)
- Live since 2017
- Limit: \$10M (raised over time)
- Coverage: ~70% of US deposit accounts

## FedNow

- Operator: Federal Reserve
- Live July 2023
- Default limit: \$1M (configurable to \$500K-\$1M)
- Open to all US banks (broader reach long-term)

## Properties

- Settlement in seconds
- Final and irrevocable
- ISO 20022 native ([[iso-20022]])
- Credit push only (no pull / debit yet)

## Use cases

- Earned wage access (payroll on demand)
- B2B instant settlement
- Insurance claim payouts
- Account-to-account consumer transfers

## Replaces / pressures

Same-day [[ach]] for urgency. Domestic [[wire]] for sub-\$1M.
