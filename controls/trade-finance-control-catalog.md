# Trade finance — control catalog

| ID | Control | Frequency | Evidence | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| C-TF-SAN-01 | Multi-list sanctions on parties + vessels + ports + commodities | Per issuance + amendment + drawing | Screening log | Sanctions breach |
| C-TF-DUL-01 | Dual-use goods + weapons screening | Per issuance | Goods classification | Reg / criminal |
| C-TF-CRD-01 | Credit line + collateral allocation | Per issuance | Limit log | Credit risk |
| C-TF-DOC-01 | Document examination within UCP 600 (5 banking days) | Per drawing | Examination log + discrepancy notes | Customer service / reg |
| C-TF-AML-01 | Trade-based money laundering (TBML) screening | Per drawing | TBML rules + alerts | Reg breach |
| C-TF-CL-01 | Contingent liability reporting | Daily/Monthly | Reporting feed | Capital + reg |
| C-TF-FEE-01 | Fee billing accuracy | Per event | Billing log | Revenue + customer dispute |
| C-TF-FRAUD-01 | Suspicious shipment + invoice over-/under-invoicing detection | Per drawing | Risk score | Fraud + AML |

## TBML risk patterns

- Over- / under-invoicing
- Phantom shipments
- Multiple invoicing same goods
- Black-market peso exchange
- Trade with high-risk jurisdictions
