# 05 — Relationship Layer

Who at the bank does what for corporate client.

## Treasury Sales Officer (TSO)

- Front office, quota-carrying, owns RFP response
- Industry vertical specialization (healthcare, tech, gov, FI)
- Comp: base + bonus on new sales + retention
- Sells [[02-products]], coordinates with credit RM

## Treasury Management Officer (TMO) / Implementation

- Project manage onboarding 3-12 months ([[01-onboarding]])
- Coordinates ops, tech ([[03-tech-integration]]), legal, credit
- SLA tracking, go-live signoff
- Hands off to service after stable

## Client Service / Customer Support

- Tier 1: portal, password resets, transaction lookups
- Tier 2: file exceptions, format issues
- Tier 3: technical (SWIFT, [[api]])
- Named service officer for big clients
- Handles [[04-daily-ops]] exceptions

## Credit / Relationship Manager (RM)

- Owns total P&L for client across products
- Cross-sell: cash mgmt + lending + capital markets + custody
- Decides credit limits, pricing exceptions ([[06-pricing-revenue]])
- Senior relationship — escalation point

## Org chart insight

Bank revenue engine = RM owns wallet share, TSO drives cash mgmt fees, TMO+Service retain client. Misaligned incentives common — RM may discount fees to win lending mandate.
