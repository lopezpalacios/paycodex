# EPC — European Payments Council

Industry body governing SEPA scheme rulebooks.

## Outputs

- SCT, SDD, SCT Inst rulebooks
- Clarification papers, FAQs
- VOP scheme rulebook

## Membership

Banks + payment service providers across SEPA zone.

## Vs [[eba]] / [[ecb-eurosystem]]

- EPC = industry self-governance for scheme rules
- EBA / ECB = regulatory + operational layers
- All three coordinate but have distinct mandates
