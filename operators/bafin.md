# BaFin — German Federal Financial Supervisory Authority

German prudential + conduct supervisor.

## For cash mgmt

- Supervises German banks providing cash-mgmt to corporates with German ops
- Local AML enforcement under [[../regulations/amld-amlr-amla]]
- Reporting layer for [[../regulations/dora]] incidents in DE

## Note

Each EU member state has equivalent (ACPR-FR, CSSF-LU, DNB-NL, AFM, MFSA-MT, etc). Pattern repeats across 27 jurisdictions.
