# SNB — Swiss National Bank

Swiss central bank. Mandate: price stability, supports CH economic policy.

## Cash-mgmt relevance

- Owns oversight of [[../concepts/sic]] (operated by [[six]])
- Settlement in central bank money on SIC
- Sets monetary policy → CHF interest rate environment
- Operates SNB account regime (banks hold sight deposits at SNB)

## History note

Negative interest rate policy 2015-2022 — drove tiered exemption thresholds for bank reserves, shaped Swiss [[../concepts/sweep]] structures.
