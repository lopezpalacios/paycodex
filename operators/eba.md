# EBA — European Banking Authority

EU regulator producing technical standards (RTS, ITS), guidelines.

## Cash-mgmt-relevant outputs

- PSD2 SCA RTS
- IPR RTS on [[../concepts/vop]]
- AML RTS under [[../regulations/amld-amlr-amla]]
- Liquidity / LCR / NSFR rules under CRR

## Vs ECB

- EBA = rules-writer, supervision via national authorities + ECB SSM
- ECB = operator + direct supervisor for significant institutions

## Note: also "EBA Clearing"

[[../concepts/eba-step2]] / [[../concepts/eba-rt1]] are operated by **EBA Clearing** — a private bank-owned company, NOT the European Banking Authority. Confusing namespace clash.
