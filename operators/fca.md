# FCA — Financial Conduct Authority (UK)

UK conduct regulator. Pairs with PRA on prudential.

## Mandate

- Conduct supervision (consumer protection, market integrity)
- AML supervision (joint with NCA)
- Authorisation of FCA-only firms (e.g., EMIs, payment firms)
- Open Banking + PSD2-equivalent rules post-Brexit

## Cash mgmt link

- E-money + payment institution authorisation
- Conduct rules on retail payments
- APP fraud reimbursement scheme (PSR mandate, FCA enforcement adjacent)
