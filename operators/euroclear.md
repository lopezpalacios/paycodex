# Euroclear

International CSD (Central Securities Depository). HQ Brussels.

## Services

- Securities settlement (DvP)
- Custody
- Collateral management ([[../concepts/repo]] tri-party)
- Corporate actions processing

## Group entities

- Euroclear Bank (ICSD)
- Euroclear UK & International (CREST — UK CSD)
- Euroclear France, Belgium, Netherlands, Finland, Sweden

## Cash mgmt link

- Treasury repo settlement via tri-party
- Securities collateral pledged for intraday RTGS credit
- T2S link via Eurosystem
