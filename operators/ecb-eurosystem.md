# ECB / Eurosystem

European Central Bank + national central banks of the eurozone. Operator of [[../concepts/t2]] and [[../concepts/tips]].

## For cash mgmt

- Sets EUR monetary policy, drives ECR levels indirectly
- Operates the EUR settlement layer
- Supervises significant banks via SSM (Single Supervisory Mechanism)
