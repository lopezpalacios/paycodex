# MROS — Money Laundering Reporting Office Switzerland

Swiss Financial Intelligence Unit. Receives Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) from banks.

## Mandate

- Receives SARs from financial intermediaries
- Analyzes + forwards to law enforcement
- Cooperates with foreign FIUs (Egmont Group)

## Swiss reporting framework

- "Right to report" (Art. 305ter Swiss Criminal Code)
- "Duty to report" (Art. 9 AMLA) — when there is reasonable suspicion
- Bank can freeze funds during MROS review
