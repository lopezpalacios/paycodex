# CLS Group / CLS Bank

PvP FX settlement utility. CLS Bank is Edge Act bank in NY, supervised by Federal Reserve.

## Services

- **CLS Settlement** — PvP for 18 currencies, ~70 settlement members
- **CLSNet** — bilateral matching/netting
- **CLSNow** — same-day liquidity service

## Why it exists

- Eliminates Herstatt risk (one-leg-paid in FX settlement)
- Settles both legs simultaneously across central bank accounts
- Reduces gross settlement to net funding

## Membership tiers

- Settlement Members (direct)
- Third Party (via member)
- ~25K third-party users
