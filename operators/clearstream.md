# Clearstream

Deutsche Börse Group's CSD. International + national securities settlement.

## Group entities

- Clearstream Banking Frankfurt (German CSD)
- Clearstream Banking Luxembourg (ICSD)
- LuxCSD

## Services

Same shape as [[euroclear]]:

- Securities settlement
- Custody
- Collateral mgmt (tri-party)
- Corporate actions

## Vs Euroclear

- Two ICSDs in EU (oligopoly)
- Bridge between them for cross-CSD settlement
- Banks typically use both depending on issuance domicile
