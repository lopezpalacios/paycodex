# PRA — Prudential Regulation Authority (UK)

UK prudential regulator. Part of [[boe]].

## Mandate

- Bank + insurer prudential supervision
- Capital + liquidity requirements (UK CRR / CRD implementation)
- Recovery + resolution planning

## Cash mgmt link

- Bank capital + liquidity rules driving deposit pricing
- Operational resilience rules (PRA SS1/21) — UK version of [[dora]]
