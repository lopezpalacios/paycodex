# FINMA — Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority

Prudential + conduct supervisor for Swiss banks, insurers, market infra.

## Mandate

- Banking license issuance + supervision
- AML supervision via [[../regulations/swiss-amla]]
- Conduct rules under [[../regulations/finsa-finia]]
- Operational risk circular (23/1) — DORA-equivalent direction

## For cash mgmt

- Approves new products affecting depositor protection
- Supervises [[../07-risk-controls]] frameworks
- Enforcement on KYC/AML failures
