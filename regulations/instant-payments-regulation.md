# Instant Payments Regulation (IPR) — EU 2024/886

EU regulation mandating SCT Inst availability + pricing parity + VOP.

## Key dates

| Obligation | Eurozone | Non-eurozone EU |
|---|---|---|
| Receive SCT Inst | Jan 9 2025 | Jan 9 2027 |
| Send SCT Inst | Oct 9 2025 | Jul 9 2027 |
| Pricing parity | Jan 9 2025 | Jan 9 2027 |
| [[vop]] mandatory | Oct 9 2025 | Jul 9 2027 |
| Daily customer sanctions screening | Jan 9 2025 | Jan 9 2027 |

## Scope

- All PSPs offering [[sepa-sct]] in EU
- Includes EMIs (extended via 2024 amendments)
- Pricing of [[sct-inst]] cannot exceed equivalent SCT
- VOP applies to ALL credit transfers, not just instant

## Sanctions paradigm shift

- Per-transaction screening impossible at <10s for instant
- Move to **daily customer-level screening** against sanctions lists
- Catch-and-block at onboarding + periodic re-screen
- Fundamental rearchitecture for many banks
