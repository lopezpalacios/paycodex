# Swiss AMLA + AMLO-FINMA

Bundesgesetz über die Bekämpfung der Geldwäscherei (AMLA) + AMLO-FINMA ordinance.

## Core obligations

- CDD on all banking relationships
- **Form A** — beneficial owner declaration (≥25% threshold for legal entities)
- **Form K** — controlling person declaration
- Risk-based approach + EDD for high-risk
- Reporting to MROS (FIU) on suspicion (note: Swiss "right to report" + "duty to report")

## Specifics vs EU [[amld-amlr-amla]]

- Long-standing tradition of bank-led KYC (vs central register evolution in EU)
- Banking secrecy heritage — eroded post-FATCA, AEOI/CRS, but still distinctive
- FINMA enforcement, MROS reporting

## Updates

- 2023 reform — beneficial ownership transparency register in train
- AEOI / CRS implementation — automatic info exchange with partner jurisdictions
