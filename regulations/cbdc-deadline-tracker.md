## CH posture

- Wholesale: leading-edge (SNB + SIX)
- Retail: explicit no near-term plans, focus stays wholesale
- Reason: CH already has robust [[../concepts/sic]] + [[../concepts/twint]], no gap CBDC fills

## EU posture

- Wholesale: research, Eurosystem exploring DLT-based settlement (some banks via Project Agorá)
- Retail: digital euro in active preparation, decision pending

## UK posture

- Wholesale: BoE Synchronisation lab, no live wholesale CBDC yet
- Retail: digital pound consultation, no decision
