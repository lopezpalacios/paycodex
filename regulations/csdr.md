# CSDR — Central Securities Depositories Regulation

EU Regulation 909/2014. Settlement discipline + CSD authorization.

## Key components

- CSD authorization + supervision regime
- Settlement Discipline Regime (SDR) — penalties + (suspended) buy-in
- Internalised settlement reporting
- T+2 (now T+1 transition by Oct 2027)

## Settlement Discipline (SDR)

In force Feb 2022:

- **Cash penalties** for late settlement (live)
- **Mandatory buy-in** (suspended indefinitely after industry pushback)

## Cash penalty regime

- Daily charge on failing party
- Per ISIN per day
- Rate by asset class (1bp/day liquid eq, 0.5bp/day illiquid bonds, etc.)
- Settled monthly via CSD penalty mechanism

## T+1 transition

- Target: 11 Oct 2027 for EU
- US already T+1 since May 2024
- Squeezes pre-matching, FX, funding workflows
- Drives investment in real-time post-trade processing

## Cash mgmt impact

- Failed settlements = cash flow disruption
- Penalties = direct P&L hit
- T+1 = tighter intraday liquidity demand
