# EU Taxonomy

EU Reg 2020/852. Classification system for environmentally sustainable economic activities.

## Six environmental objectives

1. Climate change mitigation
2. Climate change adaptation
3. Sustainable use of water + marine resources
4. Transition to circular economy
5. Pollution prevention + control
6. Protection + restoration of biodiversity + ecosystems

## Activity = "green" if

- Substantially contributes to ≥1 of 6 objectives
- Does no significant harm (DNSH) to others
- Meets minimum social safeguards
- Complies with technical screening criteria

## Cash mgmt link

- Used as basis for [[../concepts/green-deposit]] eligibility
- KPI definitions in [[../concepts/esg-linked-loan]] often reference taxonomy
- Banks report taxonomy-aligned share of book ([[csrd]] disclosure)

## Status

- Climate-related screening criteria: live since 2022
- Other 4 objectives: criteria adopted 2023, application 2024
- Continuous review
