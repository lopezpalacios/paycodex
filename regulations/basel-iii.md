# Basel III

International bank regulatory framework. Sets capital + liquidity + leverage standards. Implemented in EU via CRR / CRD; CH via FINMA Capital Adequacy Ordinance; UK via PRA rules.

## Pillars

1. **Capital adequacy** — risk-weighted asset (RWA) coverage
2. **Supervisory review** — Pillar 2 add-ons
3. **Market discipline** — Pillar 3 disclosure

## Capital ratios

- CET1 (Common Equity Tier 1): ≥4.5% RWA + buffers
- Tier 1: ≥6%
- Total: ≥8%
- Leverage ratio: ≥3% (≥3.5% for G-SIBs under Basel IV)

- [[../concepts/lcr]] — 30-day stress

## Cash mgmt impact

- Drives bank preference for operating deposits ([[../06-pricing-revenue]])
- ZBA / sweep structures preserve "operating" classification
- Non-operating wholesale deposits expensive — penalized
- Post-Basel IV (live by 2025-2028 phased): output floor on internal models, may shift product economics
