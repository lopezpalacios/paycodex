# FinSA / FinIA — Swiss financial services + institution acts

In force 2020. Swiss equivalent of MiFID II + investment firm rules.

## FinSA (Financial Services Act)

- Conduct rules at point of sale
- Client classification (retail / professional / institutional)
- Basic Information Sheet (BIB) for products
- Suitability + appropriateness checks

## FinIA (Financial Institutions Act)

- Licensing regime: portfolio managers, trustees, securities firms, fund mgmt, banks
- FINMA + supervisory organisations (SOs) for smaller firms
- FinTech license — light-touch banking license for innovation

## Cash-mgmt relevance

- Treasury solutions classified — sweep, MMF investments fall under product rules
- FX hedging products subject to FinSA conduct
- Bank cash mgmt sales staff need FinSA training + records
