# DORA — Digital Operational Resilience Act

EU Reg 2022/2554. In force from Jan 17 2025.

## Scope

All EU financial entities + their critical ICT third-party providers (CTPPs).

## Pillars

1. **ICT risk management** — board accountability, framework, policies
2. **ICT incident reporting** — major incidents to regulator within hours
3. **Digital operational resilience testing** — TLPT (threat-led penetration test), every 3 years for big firms
4. **ICT third-party risk** — register of providers, exit strategies, audit rights, CTPP designation
5. **Information sharing** — voluntary cyber threat intel exchange

## Implementation reality

- Massive contract repapering with vendors
- Register of Information (RoI) submissions to national regulators
- Often biggest 2024-25 program at large banks alongside [[instant-payments-regulation]]

## CH context

CH not bound, but Swiss banks serving EU clients via EU subs comply. CH likely to introduce equivalent under FINMA Circular 23/1 (operational risk).
