# FATCA / CRS

Tax information exchange regimes affecting account opening + reporting.

## FATCA (US)

- Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act, 2010
- Non-US banks identify US persons, report account info to IRS (via local tax authority IGA)
- W-8 / W-9 forms collected at onboarding
- 30% withholding on US-source income to non-compliant foreign FI

## CRS (OECD)

- Common Reporting Standard
- 100+ countries automatically exchange tax info on non-resident account holders
- Self-certification at onboarding (residence)
- Annual reporting per jurisdiction

## CH context

- CH signed FATCA IGA + AEOI partner of CRS
- Major shift from historic banking secrecy
- Drives KYC tax data collection effort

## Cash-mgmt-relevant points

- Onboarding adds FATCA / CRS forms
- Periodic refresh required
- Reporting to local tax authority annually
- Account closure decisions if non-cooperative
