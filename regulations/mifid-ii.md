# MiFID II / MiFIR

EU markets in financial instruments framework. In force Jan 2018.

## Scope

- Investment services + activities (advice, execution, dealing, portfolio mgmt)
- Trading venues (regulated markets, MTFs, OTFs)
- Pre/post-trade transparency
- Best execution
- Suitability + appropriateness

## Cash-mgmt-relevant points

- **Suitability** — treasury investment products (MMF, structured deposits) require client classification
- **Best execution** — bank must demonstrate fair pricing on FX, deposits beyond simple cash
- **Inducements** — restrictions on commissions, soft dollars (think [[../concepts/ecr]] in EU context)
- **Transaction reporting** — applicable to derivatives ([[emir]] adjacency)

## Client classification

- Retail (most protection)
- Professional (per se or elective)
- Eligible Counterparty (least protection, financial-only)
