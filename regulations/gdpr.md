# GDPR

EU Reg 2016/679. Personal data protection. In force May 2018.

## Cash-mgmt-relevant points

- IBAN, account name, transaction data = personal data when linked to natural person
- Lawful basis for processing — usually contract performance + legal obligation (AML)
- Data minimization tension with VOP / sanctions screening
- Cross-border transfer rules — EU↔CH adequacy in place, EU↔US under Data Privacy Framework

## DPIA required for

- Large-scale fraud monitoring
- Behavioral analytics on payment patterns
- Profiling for credit / risk scoring

## Breach notification

- 72 hours to supervisory authority
- Affected data subjects "without undue delay" if high risk

## CH equivalent

[[fadp]] — revised Swiss FADP, in force Sept 2023, GDPR-aligned but with quirks (no GDPR-style fines initially).
