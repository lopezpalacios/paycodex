# AMLD / AMLR / AMLA — EU AML framework

## Stack

- **AMLD** — Anti-Money Laundering Directive (5 + 6 in force, more amending)
- **AMLR** — single AML Regulation (directly applicable, replaces patchwork)
- **AMLA** — Authority for Anti-Money Laundering, Frankfurt-based, started 2025
- Package adopted June 2024, phased application 2025-2027

## Key requirements

- Customer Due Diligence (CDD) standardized
- Beneficial ownership ≥25% (with member-state add-ons)
- PEP screening
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) to national FIUs
- Sanctions integration ([[../concepts/ofac]] + EU consolidated list)

## Why AMLA matters

- First EU-level direct supervisor for selected obliged entities
- Pan-EU consistency vs current national-supervisor patchwork
- Targets large cross-border banks + crypto
