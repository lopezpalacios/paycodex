# Digital Euro — proposed regulation

EU regulation establishing legal framework for [[../concepts/digital-euro]]. Commission proposal June 2023.

## Components

- **Single Currency Package**:
  - Regulation establishing digital euro
  - Regulation on cash legal tender (parallel)

## Key provisions (as proposed)

- Digital euro = legal tender, on par with banknotes
- Issuance by ECB, distributed by credit institutions + EMIs
- Holding limit (likely €3K, exact figure under negotiation)
- Privacy: low-value offline transactions anonymous
- Mandatory distribution by significant credit institutions
- Compensation for distributing PSPs (mechanism debated)

## Status

- In trilogue / negotiation 2024-2025
- Adoption target: 2026
- ECB go-ahead decision separate, could come 2026-2027
- Earliest issuance: 2027-2028

## Cash mgmt impact

- New product line for banks (wallets, exchange, custody)
- Liquidity model change (CBDC outflow risk)
- AML / sanctions integration with new asset class
- Interoperability with existing cash mgmt rails
