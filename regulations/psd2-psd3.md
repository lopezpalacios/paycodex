# PSD2 / PSD3 / PSR

EU Payment Services Directive. Foundation for retail payments rules + open banking.

## PSD2 (in force since 2018)

- Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) — 3DS2, two-factor
- Open Banking — AISP (account info), PISP (payment init), CBPII (card-based)
- Regulated TPPs, dedicated PSP API or modified customer interface fallback
- Liability shift on unauthorized payments

## PSD3 + PSR (in flight)

- Proposed 2023, expected ~2026 application
- PSR = directly applicable Regulation (no national transposition)
- Tightens APIs (kills "modified customer interface" fallback)
- Strengthens fraud rules — IBAN/name check, scam liability
- Aligns with [[instant-payments-regulation]]

## CH context

PSD2 not directly binding in CH — Swiss banks doing EU business comply via subsidiaries. CH has own FinTech licensing path ([[finsa-finia]]).
