# SFDR — Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation

EU 2019/2088. Disclosure rules for financial market participants on sustainability of financial products.

## Scope

- AIFMs, UCITS managers, insurance providers, pension providers, banks providing portfolio mgmt + advice
- Pre-contractual + periodic + website disclosures

## Article classification

- **Article 6** — generic financial product, no sustainability claims
- **Article 8** — promotes ESG characteristics ("light green")
- **Article 9** — sustainable investment objective ("dark green")

## Cash mgmt link

- Banks selling treasury investment products (MMFs, structured deposits) classify under SFDR
- Reporting requirements per article level
- Marketing materials must reflect classification

## Status

- Level 1 (the regulation): applicable since 2021
- Level 2 (RTS): applicable since Jan 2023
- Review in flight 2024-2026
