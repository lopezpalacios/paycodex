# EMIR — European Market Infrastructure Regulation

EU regulation on OTC derivatives, CCPs, trade repositories. In force 2012, refit 2019, EMIR 3.0 in train.

## Scope

- OTC derivatives (rates, FX, credit, equity, commodities)
- Central counterparties (CCPs)
- Trade repositories

## Key obligations

- **Clearing obligation** — standardized OTC derivatives clear via CCP (LCH, Eurex, ICE)
- **Reporting** — every derivative trade reported to TR within T+1
- **Risk mitigation** — non-cleared: portfolio reconciliation, dispute resolution, collateral exchange (initial + variation margin)

## Cash mgmt link

- FX hedging trades may fall under EMIR (FX forwards >7d, FX options)
- Variation margin = additional cash collateral flow daily
- Repo via CCP (Eurex Repo, LCH RepoClear) under EMIR clearing
- Drives daily margin call infrastructure for treasury

## EMIR 3.0 (in flight)

- Active account requirement at EU CCP (push business away from UK CCPs post-Brexit)
- Phased application 2025-2027
