# FADP — Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (revised)

In force Sept 1 2023. GDPR-aligned with Swiss specifics.

## Vs [[gdpr]]

- Aligned: lawful basis, data subject rights, breach notification, DPO concept
- Different: no automatic fines — penalties are criminal liability of natural persons (up to CHF 250k)
- Stricter: explicit consent for high-risk profiling, data export rules
- Adequacy with EU maintained

## For cash mgmt

- Same DPIA expectations as GDPR
- Cross-border transfers EU↔CH straightforward (adequacy)
- Cross-border CH↔US needs SCCs or DPF equivalent
